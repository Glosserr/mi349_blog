About the Blogger/Author

Hello! I'm Joey Glosser, a passionate web developer, computer scientist, and carnivorous plant hobbyist.

With 5 years in computer science related fields, I've had the privilege of working on all of my interests as I have happened upon them.

Since quarantine I have had learned game development, app development, cybersecurity and of course web design. With these areas in mind, my skills include:

  • Front-end development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
  • Back-end development (Node.js, Python)
  • Games Codebase Development (C#, Unity, Unreal)
  • Cybersecurity Progams Developer (Python, C++, SQL)
  • Mobile app development (React Native)

When I taking a break from software design, I tend to my carnivorous plants, read manga, watch anime, and work on 3D modelling for 3D printing.

I believe in lifelong learning and am always excited to take on new challenges and opportunities in the ever-evolving world of technology.

Through this blog, I hope to share my experiences, and insights with fellow developers and tech enthusiasts. With a bit of a mix between the tech space, and that of carnivorous and exotic horticulture!
